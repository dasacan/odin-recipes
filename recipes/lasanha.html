Receita de lasanha

Receita fácil e rápida, tempo total: 30 minutos

Esta é a receita original do ep 2 do seriado "Cozinho fácil" é a mais votada da Internet!!

This is a great recipe when you don't want to wait for the dough to rise. You just mix it and allow it to rest for 5 minutes and then it's ready to go! It yields a soft, chewy crust.

Ingredientes:

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 ½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
